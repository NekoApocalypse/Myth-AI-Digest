**The Noise**

The AI advice study is getting outsized attention for what's essentially "people trust a tool they were told to trust." The setup (gimped model + trivia questions) tests automation bias, not anything specific to LLMs. The cognitive surrender pattern is real and worth tracking, but this paper doesn't isolate AI as an independent variable. Calling the headline misleading is fair.
