# Daily AI Digest — 2026-04-08

## 🔥 The Headline

**Claude Mythos Preview + Project Glasswing** — Anthropic just disclosed that their unreleased frontier model can autonomously find and exploit zero-days in every major OS and browser, and instead of quietly sitting on that, they've corralled AWS, Apple, Google, Microsoft, NVIDIA and ~40 critical infrastructure orgs into a coordinated defensive operation. This is the most consequential capability disclosure since GPT-4; the window before hostile actors catch up is the only variable left.

---

## 📦 The Stack

**[Frontier Models] Project Glasswing — Anthropic is burning $100M in Mythos Preview credits to let Apple, Google, Microsoft and 40+ infra orgs defensively scan their codebases before the offense side catches up.** Mythos already found thousands of high-severity vulns including a 27-year-old OpenBSD bug; it chains four browser vulns into a full sandbox escape overnight with no human in the loop. <https://www.anthropic.com/glasswing>

**[Frontier Models] Claude Mythos Preview Cybersecurity Assessment — The red team receipts:** autonomous 20-gadget ROP chains over multiple packets for FreeBSD NFS RCE, JIT heap sprays escaping renderer + OS sandboxes, and Linux LPE via KASLR bypass—all built by engineers with zero formal security training. Over 99% of found vulnerabilities are still unpatched. <https://red.anthropic.com/2026/mythos-preview/>

**[Agents] GLM-5.1: Towards Long-Horizon Tasks — Z.ai drops a 744B-parameter MIT-licensed open-source model** that doesn't plateau after initial gains—it sustains optimization over hundreds of rounds and thousands of tool calls. Ranks #1 open-source on Vending Bench 2 and leads on SWE-Bench Pro. Full vLLM/SGLang/KTransformers support, FP8 quantized, on HuggingFace now. <https://github.com/zai-org/GLM-5>

**[Agents] Google Scion — Open-source multi-agent orchestration where each agent gets its own container, git worktree, and credentials.** Harness-agnostic (Claude Code, Gemini CLI, Codex), runs on Docker/Kubernetes, and lets the models themselves coordinate via a shared CLI tool rather than prescribing rigid patterns. Early but the architecture is clean. <https://github.com/GoogleCloudPlatform/scion>

**[Hardware] Gemma 4 Multimodal Fine-Tuner for Apple Silicon — The only toolkit that fine-tunes Gemma 4 on all three modalities (text + image + audio) natively on MPS.** Unsloth and Axolotl drop you at audio; this one doesn't. Streams from GCS/BigQuery so you're not copying terabytes to your laptop. Fills a real gap. <https://github.com/mattmireles/gemma-tuner-multimodal>
