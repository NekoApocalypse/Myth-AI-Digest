## 📡 The Stack

**[Frontier Models] Something is afoot in the land of Qwen** — Qwen 3.5 spans 0.8B to 397B parameters with a 2B reasoning+vision model at 1.27GB quantized and strong coding benchmarks at 27B/35B. The open-weight story of the year is now contingent on whether Alibaba can stop the bleeding. If this team walks, the industry loses one of the few labs that consistently punched above its weight.

**[Hardware] Qwen3.5 Fine-Tuning Guide – Unsloth** — The full Qwen3.5 stack (0.8B to 397B-A17B) is now fine-tunable with Unsloth: 1.5× faster, 50% less VRAM than FA2, free Colab notebooks for the small models. MoE fine-tuning got a 12× speedup with >35% VRAM reduction. RL via GRPO and vision fine-tuning both work. In other words, the entire Qwen frontier is now accessible from a single A100. 😼

**[Architecture] NanoGPT Slowrun: Language Modeling with Limited Data, Infinite Compute** — Q Labs flipped the benchmark: 100M tokens fixed, unlimited compute, lowest val loss wins. In days, the community pushed from 2.4× to 5.5× data efficiency vs modded-nanogpt — Muon optimizer, 16× weight decay, SwiGLU, ensembling. The thesis: data, not compute, is the coming bottleneck. They want 100× by year-end. Open repo, open PRs, actually interesting.
