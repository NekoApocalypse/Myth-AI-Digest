**The Headline**
Google shipped Gemini 3.5 Flash with frontier agentic performance at 4x the speed of competing frontier models, alongside Gemini Omni for multimodal creation — the real story is the Antigravity subagent harness and Spark personal agents hitting consumer scale.

**The Stack**
- [Frontier Models] **Gemini 3.5 Flash** — Outperforms Gemini 3.1 Pro on Terminal-Bench 2.1 (76.2%) and MCP Atlas (83.6%) while delivering 4x output tokens per second. Powers 24/7 Gemini Spark personal agents and the Antigravity subagent harness. <https://blog.google/innovation-and-ai/models-and-research/gemini-models/gemini-3-5/>
- [Agents] **Forge** — Reliability layer for self-hosted LLM tool-calling that lifts an 8B model to 86.5% on multi-step agentic workflows through guardrails (rescue parsing, retry nudges, step enforcement) and VRAM-aware context compaction. Drop-in OpenAI-compatible proxy. <https://github.com/antoinezambelli/forge>
- [Frontier Models] **Gemini Omni** — Multimodal creation model combining reasoning with video editing, object replacement, style transfer, and complex generative video. Includes SynthID watermarking and C2PA Content Credentials. <https://deepmind.google/models/gemini-omni/>
- [Breakout] **OpenAI Adopts SynthID + Public Verification Tool** — Multi-layered provenance: Google DeepMind's invisible watermarking for images, C2PA metadata conformance, and a public verification tool (openai.com/verify) to detect AI-generated content. <https://openai.com/index/advancing-content-provenance/>
- [Breakout] **TLA+ for the LLM Era (Specula)** — Using frontier LLMs to generate formal verification specs that catch bugs missed by standard agentic review flows. Includes open-source Specula tool. <https://emptysqua.re/blog/intro-to-tla-plus-for-the-llm-era/>
