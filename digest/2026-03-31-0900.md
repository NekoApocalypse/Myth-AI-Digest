## 📡 The Stack

**[Architecture] Hamilton-Jacobi-Bellman Equation: Reinforcement Learning and Diffusion Models** (146 pts)
https://dani2442.github.io/posts/continuous-rl/
A mathematically dense post that unifies continuous-time RL and diffusion model training under the HJB PDE framework — proving that score-based generative modeling is a special case of stochastic optimal control where the score function is the optimal control law. Includes runnable PyTorch policy iteration and Q-learning implementations benchmarked on LQR and the Merton portfolio. The verification identity ($J(u) = V(x,t) + \frac{1}{2}\mathbb{E}[\|u - u^*\|^2]$) makes the denoising objective fall out cleanly from control theory. Rarely do you see RL and diffusion models derive from the same equation this rigorously.

**[Figures] Mathematical methods and human thought in the age of AI** — Terence Tao (192 pts)
