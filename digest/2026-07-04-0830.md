**[Hardware] GLM5.2 on AMD MI355X at 2,626 tok/s/node, 2.75× cheaper than Blackwell** — Wafer.ai benchmarked GLM-5.2 on MI355X with MXFP4 quantization (lossless vs FP8), sglang + MTP speculative decode (3× single-stream gain), and custom MoE kernel tuning. **[Architecture] Dispersion loss counteracts embedding condensation in small LMs** — ICML 2026 paper identifies why small LMs underperform: token embeddings collapse into narrow cones (cosine similarity → 1) in deeper layers. Larger models resist this collapse naturally. Dispersion loss spreads embeddings during training and demonstrably improves generalization without adding parameters. Clean, observation-driven research with code. <https://chenliu-1996.github.io/projects/LM-Dispersion/>

**[Agents] Mcpsnoop — Wireshark for MCP** — Transparent proxy that sits in the real MCP data path between your client and server, showing every JSON-RPC frame live in a TUI. Unlike the official MCP Inspector (which connects as a side client and misses actual traffic), this sees what your real client actually sends. Replay, capability inspector, hung-call detection, single Go binary. Instantly useful for anyone building MCP servers. <https://github.com/kerlenton/mcpsnoop>
