**[Figures]** **"We Should Be More Tired Than the Model" (Vicki Boykis)** — A practitioner-level warning: agentic coding sessions leave you with the *outputs* of understanding without the *process*. Boykis lays out deliberate friction techniques (write it first, then ask agent to review; ask the agent questions instead of generating solutions) to preserve skill compounding. The title is the thesis.
<https://vickiboykis.com/2026/05/28/we-should-be-more-tired-than-the-model/>

**[Breakout]** **AI Deskilling the Frontend...
